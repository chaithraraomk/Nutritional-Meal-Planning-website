Vegetable Stir Fry

A quick and easy stir-fry loaded with colorful veggies.

Ingredients

  • Bell peppers, carrots, and broccoli
  • Soy sauce & garlic
  • Sesame oil
  • Tofu or chicken (optional)

Instructions

Stir-fry everything together for 10 minutes and serve.
