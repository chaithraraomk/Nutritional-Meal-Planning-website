Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cups fresh lettuce
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Grill chicken breasts until fully cooked.
  2. Chop lettuce, tomatoes, and mix with feta cheese.
  3. Slice the grilled chicken and place on top of the salad.
  4. Drizzle olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  5. Enjoy your healthy meal!
