Grilled Chicken Salad

A nutritious and delicious salad packed with protein and vitamins.

Ingredients

  • 2 grilled chicken breasts
  • 1 cup mixed greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Olive oil & lemon dressing

Instructions

Mix all ingredients together and serve fresh.
